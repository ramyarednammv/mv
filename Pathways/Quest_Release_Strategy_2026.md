## REVENUE FLOW MODEL

```
Quests (Membership) → Summit → Mastery

Week 1-3: Free/Membership Quests
         ↓ Warm audience to topic
Week 4: Summit Launch
         ↓ $5,000,000 revenue events
Week 5-6: Mastery Program
         ↓ High-ticket continuation
Ongoing: Quest engagement retains members
```

This creates a flywheel:
1. **Quests drive retention** (members stay for new content)
2. **Quests warm up topics** (educate before selling)
3. **Summits convert warm audiences** (higher conversion)
4. **Masteries capture high-intent buyers** (premium revenue)
5. **Cycle repeats** (next topic)
