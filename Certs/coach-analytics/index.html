Vishen's NL: Punching Above Its Weight

Vishen's Newsletter achieves 17.6% OR on the hardest segment of the Coach list — people not on any other Mindvalley list. Why Coaching NL Was Discontinued

Section 12

Strategic Insights & Recommendations

1

Stop Treating Coach as a Broadcast List

91% of Coach emails are invite sequences for events that aren't coaching-specific. The list's best performance comes from CLC launches (21-26% OR) and coaching challenges (21.6% OR). Generic summits drag the average down to 12-14% OR. Consider reducing non-coaching campaign sends to protect list health.

2

Vishen's Newsletter Is the Engagement Anchor

With 39 emails at 17.6% OR and the highest CTR of any recurring content (0.27%), Vishen's Newsletter is the most reliable engagement driver on the Coach list. It outperforms summit invites, sales sequences, and even the now-discontinued Coaching Newsletter on click-through rate. Protect this send — it's the floor that keeps the list warm.

3

Address the List Decay Problem

The Coach list has lost 136K subscribers (-23.9%) since April 2025. At the current rate, the list will drop below 400K by mid-2026. Recommend: (1) run a re-engagement campaign for dormant subs, (2) implement a sunset policy for 90-day inactives, (3) add new Coach acquisition flows to replenish the list.

4

Optimize Subject Line Strategy

Top performers use curiosity gaps ("I followed every rule. But..."), identity triggers ("You're Meant to be a Life Coach"), and urgency ("your free pass is about to expire"). Bottom performers use income claims ("She made $10,000") and generic urgency ("[12 hours left]"). Avoid hype; lean into professional identity.

5

Reduce High-Volume Months

The highest-volume months (Jul: 25 emails, Sep: 29 emails) had the worst open rates (12.9%, 16.1%). There's a clear inverse correlation between send volume and engagement. Cap Coach sends at 4/week maximum during non-launch periods to protect deliverability and engagement.

6

CLC Is the Franchise — Double Down

CLC campaigns are the only ones that consistently break 20% OR on the Coach list across both years (2025: 21.7%, 2026: 21.0%). This audience was acquired for coaching certification — serve that intent. Pre-launch nurture sequences, alumni stories, and certification prep content would extend the CLC engagement window beyond launch weeks.
